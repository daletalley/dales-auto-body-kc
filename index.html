About Us

Welcome to Dale's Auto Body KC, a family-run, black-owned business in Kansas City. For over 35 years, we've dedicated ourselves to providing exceptional auto body services. Our journey, spanning over five decades, is built on hard work and a commitment to unmatched service.

Our skilled team, with years of combined experience, brings a unique blend of expertise and personal touch to every repair and custom job. At Dale's Auto Body KC, you're not just a customer, but a part of our extended family. We're grateful for your trust and look forward to many more years of serving the community with passion and excellence.
